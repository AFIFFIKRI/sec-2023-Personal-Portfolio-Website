about us

Our Story

At Muslim Food Restaurants, we combine the best tastes of Asia with well-preserved recipes and “the breath of the wok” (wok hei) – passed down through generations, to create the distinctive taste of our delicacies. Every dish we serve is bursting with bold colours, textures, and flavours – ensuring every mouthful is a delight. You can visit our store for takeaway, or we can come to you via delivery or our catering service. Providing ‘halal’ and tasty food, all are welcomed to visit and have a good time.

Whatever event you have in mind; we’d love to realise it and help you welcome your guests! Whether small or big, we can work something out to cater to all your needs. We be the caterer; you be the host – spend more time with people who matter most.
